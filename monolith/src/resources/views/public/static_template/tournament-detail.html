About Tournament

The UEFA Champions League is an annual club football competition organised by the Union of European Football Associations (UEFA) and contested by top-division European clubs, deciding the competition winners through a round robin group stage to qualify for a double-legged knockout format, and a single leg final.

It is one of the most prestigious football tournaments in the world and the most prestigious club competition in European football, played by the national league champions of their national associations.

Tournament Format

  • Group Stage: 32 teams divided into 8 groups
  • Round of 16: Top 2 teams from each group
  • Quarter-finals and Semi-finals: Two-legged ties
  • Final: Single match at neutral venue
